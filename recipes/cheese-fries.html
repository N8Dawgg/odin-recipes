Cheese Fries

  • Block of Cheese of choice
  • Fries From the Store
  • Green Onions
  • Bacon 2 Strips
  1. Shred the block of cheese, making at least a 1/4 cup of shredded cheese. Do not use shredded cheese from a bag as that cheese is coated in a material that prevents it from melting.
  2. Chop the green onion until you have an appropriate amount for a garnish.
  3. Cook the bacon on the stove until it is slightly under cooked.
  4. Chop the bacon into bits.
  5. Begin cooking the fries as directed, either in an oven or in an air frier.
  6. 3-5 minutes before the fries are finished, quickly pull them out and throw the other ingredients on top.
  7. Finish cooking the fries as directed and you're good to go. Don't forget to let it cool off!
